Cody Clark

Clarinets | Saxes | Flutes | Bassoon

Cody Clark is a Seattle-based freelance musician specializing in woodwinds. Primarily a clarinetist, Cody also doubles on saxes and flutes and the bassoon in a pinch. The name "Altissimo Noises" is a nod to those high, squeaky notes that keep things exciting!
